How this works
Smart review shows only the cards that are actually due — new ones plus anything you're due to see again. Mark a card Got it and it climbs a level, so it comes back less often (1 → 3 → 7 → 16 days). Miss it with Study again and it drops back to daily until it sticks. Your progress saves in this browser, so each session picks up where the last left off.

Shuffle all ignores the schedule and runs through everything you've selected — good for a cram before a practical. No streaks, no guilt: just the cards, spaced the way memory actually works.
